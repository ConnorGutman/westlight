EVENTS

Expansive views of Brooklyn and Manhattan surround Westlight with floor-to-ceiling windows encircling the central bar and lounge. Designed by Studio Munge, the interior features black-and-white tiled floors juxtaposed by yellow and grey-colored seating, brass accents and a honed stone bar topped with

metal mesh shelving. Cozy couch seating surrounds an indoor fireplace, with high-top tables and additional lounge seats for up to 80 guests inside. Balcony seating for up to 100 guests fills an impressive wrap-around balcony to take in views of the Manhattan, Brooklyn and Queens skylines.
